# Clickzolo Docs

Public documentation and educational notes around click fraud protection, Google Ads traffic quality, invalid clicks, and cleaner paid acquisition workflows.

This repository is intentionally simple. It is meant to give advertisers, agencies, and research-driven buyers a public-safe place to understand the topic without exposing any private product code, internal infrastructure, or sensitive implementation details.

## What Clickzolo focuses on

- detecting suspicious paid traffic patterns
- reducing wasted Google Ads spend
- helping teams review low-quality click activity faster
- supporting cleaner exclusion workflows
- improving documentation for refund and traffic-quality review processes

## Who this is for

- Google Ads advertisers
- agencies managing paid traffic
- ecommerce teams protecting margin
- lead generation teams watching click quality closely
- buyers comparing click fraud protection tools

## Why this repo exists

This repo supports public education and brand discovery. It is not a source-code repo, and it does not publish anything that would compromise platform security or reveal proprietary detection logic.

If you want to see the live product, visit [Clickzolo](https://clickzolo.com/).

## Public-safe topics this repo can cover over time

- what click fraud means in Google Ads
- how invalid clicks affect campaign efficiency
- how advertisers think about refund evidence
- industry-specific click fraud patterns
- product comparison and buyer-research content
- traffic-quality monitoring concepts

## Important note

This repository does **not** include:
